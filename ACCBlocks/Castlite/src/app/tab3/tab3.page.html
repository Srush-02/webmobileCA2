About Us

STLITE AAC Blocks are a next-generation, lightweight, and high-performance building material, designed to meet modern construction demands with sustainability and strength.

Eco-Friendly Produced using fly ash and an advanced manufacturing process, STLITE AAC blocks are ideal for green buildings. Lightweight & Strong Three times lighter than traditional red bricks, yet stronger and easier to handle, reducing construction load and time. Customizable Blocks can be tailored in size and density — down to 500 kg/m³ — to suit the needs of any project. Durable & Insulating High thermal insulation and durability make STLITE AAC Blocks ideal for long-lasting and energy-efficient buildings.
